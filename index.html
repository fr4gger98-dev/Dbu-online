Bachelor of Business Administration

Key Features of the Program

BBA is such a course, which provides the graduates with those skills that translate across different fields. It will give the scope to understand the National and International Markets of various different industries like Banking, Logistics, Finance, Accounting including Human Resources. This course is a learning guide to the student in which a concrete foundation is laid to understand all aspects of Business, including management, economics, communication, accounting and last but not the least the Marketing part too. Curriculum at DBU is designed in such a way that it will help the learner to understand the business competencies and acquire professional abilities, pick the knowledge of Business principles and leadership. They will be well aware of analyzing financial statements and making strategies to develop business in both National and International Markets, by making use of data analytics and business technology.

Outcome of the Program

Student will have a myriad of encouraging and enterprising opportunities soon after their course is completed. BBA will enhance the skills and knowledge required to plunge into the dynamic world of Business. Your goals can be achieved by placing yourself in any of the fields of your interest, such as Marketing, Financial consultant, a hardcore HR manager or even better to be a visionary Entrepreneur. You can excel in your career in a short span of time, provided you focus on your goals and make best use of the learning.

MA History

MA History

Duration: 2 Years / 4 Semesters

Eligibility: Graduation in any stream with 45% marks in aggregate for general category & 40% for SC/ST/OBC or 45% in relevant subject from a recognized University.

MA History

Key Features of the Program

An MA in History provides in-depth study of various historical periods, regions, and themes, offering both broad and specialized knowledge. The program emphasizes research skills, including primary source analysis and historiography, with many requiring a thesis or dissertation. Students develop critical thinking and communication skills, often through seminars and research projects. Interdisciplinary approaches, flexible learning options (such as full-time, part-time, or online), and career development opportunities, including internships and networking, are also key features. Overall, it prepares graduates for careers in education, research, public history, and beyond.

Outcome of the Program

The outcome of an MA in History is to equip students with advanced research, analytical, and critical thinking skills, enabling them to interpret and analyze historical events and sources with depth and precision. Graduates gain the ability to write and present complex historical arguments clearly and persuasively. They are prepared for careers in academia, research, public history, museums, archives, and other fields that require strong analytical and communication skills.

Job Opportunities

History teachers or university lecturers, research positions in academic institutions, think tanks, or cultural organizations, museums, archives, heritage sites, media, publishing, historical consulting, government, policy analysis, or NGOs.

Semester PaymentINR 64,000 / 4 = INR 16,000/-

MA Political Science

MA Political Science

Duration: 2 Years / 4 Semesters

Eligibility: Graduation in any stream with 45% marks in aggregate for general category & 40% for SC/ST/OBC or 45% in relevant subject from a recognized University.

MA Political Science

Key Features of the Program

The objective of the Master of Arts in Political Science, designed for open and distance learning, is to provide students an advanced level of understanding of political systems, decision making processes and procedures, institutions and policies at all levels of government. It focuses on political ideas of Indian and western thinkers, comparative politics, international relations, political theory, social movements and state politics in India, peace and conflict in international politics etc. This M.A programme consists of 9 compulsory courses, taught over 2 years.

Outcome of the Program

The outcome of an MA in Political Science is to equip students with advanced knowledge of political systems, theory, and international relations, alongside strong analytical and research skills. Graduates gain the ability to critically evaluate political institutions, policies, and global issues, preparing them for careers in government, public policy, international organizations, non-profits, political consultancy, and research.

Job Opportunities

Policy analysts, legislative assistants, diplomats, roles with international organizations, non-profits, political consultancy firms, advocacy groups, political journalism, public relations, and research positions in think tanks or academic institutions.

Program FeeINR 48,000
Semester PaymentINR 48,000 / 4 = INR 12,000/-
Examination FeeINR 1,500/- (Per Semester)

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about admissions, fees and how online classes work.

1 Is DBU Online UGC approved? ›
Yes, DBU Online is a UGC-approved online university authorised to offer online degree programs in India. All degrees awarded are fully recognised nationally and internationally.
2 What are the BBA fees and eligibility? ›
The Online BBA is a 3-year program with an indicative annual fee of ₹24,000 (₹18,000 tuition + ₹6,000 exam/enrollment), totalling around ₹72,000 for the full program. Eligibility: 10+2 / HSC from any recognised board. Scholarships and easy EMI options are available — confirm exact figures with our admission counsellors.
3 What are the MBA fees and eligibility? ›
The Online MBA is a 2-year program with an indicative annual fee of ₹39,000 (₹32,000 tuition + ₹7,000 exam/enrollment), totalling around ₹78,000 for the full program. Eligibility: Bachelor's degree from any recognised university with at least 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved category).
4 What are the MCA fees and eligibility? ›
The Online MCA is a 2-year program with an indicative annual fee of ₹39,000 (₹32,000 tuition + ₹7,000 exam/enrollment). Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field with at least 50% marks (45% for reserved category).
5 How are online classes conducted? ›
Classes are delivered through an interactive Learning Management System (LMS) with recorded lectures, live sessions, e-content, practice tests, and virtual academic support available 24/7.
6 Is the online degree valid for jobs and higher studies? ›
Yes. Degrees awarded by DBU Online are valid for employment and higher education in India and abroad. The university is UGC-entitled and NAAC accredited.
7 Are EMI or loan options available? ›
Yes! You can choose from loan assistance, easy EMI plans, and several flexible payment options. Contact our counsellors at admission@dbuonline.in or +91 98765 43210 for personalised support.
